Why do most innovations these days not happen in India? Why are the biggest technology firms based out of the west? Why do people of the Indian origin perform so well in the west? Large number of these observations boiled down to one simple hypothesis - The education system in India. One where the syllabus is determined by the questions marked in the textbook and the best student is the one who knows the book by heart instead of the one who is truly inquisitive. Disturbed by these observations, Sunali Gandhi born, brought up and educated in Mumbai decided to take a small step by voluntarily providing educational aids to some of the students in CBSE schools in Ahmedabad

As awareness about the quality of CBSE education (NCERT syllabus) increased, more and more students sought the help of Sunali Ma'am dissatisfied with the education quality in their schools

With exponentially increasing number of students, a need was felt to step out to a more central location which was designed for learning and more convenient for the students. Sunali's Classes was born to a larger world in Jaydeep Complex. Right from the start, we have believed in using the best technology to aid learning and we pioneered using Smart Interactive Board at our class for engaging studying

As more & more batches passed out of Sunali's Classes with incredible results - not just in Board exams but also in the test of the real world, the demand for Sunali's Classes grew multifold. Social Studies & English were also added to the fold.

To be more accessible to larger set of students, the education area of the classes was almost doubled every year since then till today and yet, the quest for real knowledge was visible by how soon our seats got filled

Strengthening the original vision of the classes, a major milestone was achieved when an exclusive Mobile App was launched specifically to encourage students to ask doubts and get utmost clarity - strengthening the original vision - #DoubtsAchheHain. The application has been a platform where tens of thousands of doubts sessions have been successfully completed

We now have 6 separate class areas with dedicated space for focused learning & revision for students. A faculty strength of ~15 which have been handpicked for their experience & quality of teaching. We now teach 8-10th Standard students for Maths, Science, Social Studies & English for CBSE & NCERT boards. And yet this is just the beginning. We are in the process of launching a revolutionary & proprietary online platform to personalize learning for the students in terms of learning cards, tests & report cards. Stay tuned for more updates.

Why us?

Some of the reasons you might consider choosing us...

Expert CBSE & NCERT Faculty

Specialized expert faculties for respective subjects. Smart teaching methodology (videos, illustrations & demos) for our smart students. We strive to develop students’ mindset and nurture their analytical thinking to be ready for competitive exams.

Rigorous Training

Daily class (Monday to Saturday) for Maths and Science for seamless studying. Weekly 10+1 hours of rigorous teaching sessions for Maths and Science & 4 hours for Social Studies & English. Tests shall be conducted regularly and results shall be communicated immediately.

24/7 Learning

Our proprietary online platform - INQ enables students to learn anytime from anywhere, take tests & ask doubts. We believe that #DoubtsAchheHein & encourage our students to ask doubts any time on our specially designed Android app – Sunali’s Classes (Google Play Store). In case, student requires interaction with faculty for doubt resolution, that can be done with prior appointment

Personalized Tutoring

Every child is special for us! Students will be given all the required extra & special attention. Advanced level of practice sheets and training for students who wish to put in even more effort. Personalized Dashboards & Report cards for students to identify their key strengths & improvement areas
