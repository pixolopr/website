Why us?

Some of the reasons you might consider choosing us...

Expert CBSE & NCERT Faculty

Specialized expert faculties for respective subjects. Smart teaching methodology (videos, illustrations & demos) for our smart students. We strive to develop students’ mindset and nurture their analytical thinking to be ready for competitive exams.

Rigorous Training

Daily class (Monday to Saturday) for Maths and Science for seamless studying. Weekly 10+1 hours of rigorous teaching sessions for Maths and Science & 4 hours for Social Studies & English. Tests shall be conducted regularly and results shall be communicated immediately.

24/7 Learning

Our proprietary online platform - INQ enables students to learn anytime from anywhere, take tests & ask doubts. We believe that #DoubtsAchheHein & encourage our students to ask doubts any time on our specially designed Android app – Sunali’s Classes (Google Play Store). In case, student requires interaction with faculty for doubt resolution, that can be done with prior appointment

Personalized Tutoring

Every child is special for us! Students will be given all the required extra & special attention. Advanced level of practice sheets and training for students who wish to put in even more effort. Personalized Dashboards & Report cards for students to identify their key strengths & improvement areas
